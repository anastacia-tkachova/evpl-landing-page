How to Play

Organize Delivery Items Through Triple Matching

  • Find Matching Objects

    Search for three identical 3D items across puzzle boards.

  • Clear Delivery Spaces

    Remove all objects to complete level objectives.

  • Use Helpful Boosters

    Hints and special tools assist during difficult stages.

  • Unlock New Collections

    Discover fresh object categories and new delivery environments.

  • Improve Matching Speed

    The faster you spot matches, the easier it becomes to clear levels.
