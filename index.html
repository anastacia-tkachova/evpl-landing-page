About the Game

It's basically a puzzle game
 where you sort stuff -
 pretty addictive once you get into it

We basically took organizing stuff - which is usually boring as hell - and somehow made it actually fun - you're hunting for matching items in these cluttered delivery areas, and the puzzles get pretty creative as you go. It's all about focus and spotting patterns quickly, plus it looks really good.

Anyone can jump in and play for a few minutes on their phone.

When you finish a level, there's this nice sense of everything being in its place. Everything should look polished and colorful, with that modern mobile game feel.
